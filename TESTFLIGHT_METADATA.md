## Beta App Description
Infuse Pro is the all-in-one platform for mobile IV therapy — built for both patients and the companies who serve them.

For patients: Book IV hydration and wellness drips delivered to your home, office, or hotel. Track your appointment in real time, message your tech, view your treatment history, and earn loyalty rewards — all in one place.

For mobile IV companies: A complete operations platform. Patient booking, dispatch, tech management, electronic charting, NP medical oversight, and HIPAA-compliant communication.

Premium mobile IV therapy, delivered to you.
